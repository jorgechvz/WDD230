Discover Arequipa

"Learn more about our white city"

Arequipa is a city that stands out for its strong presence in the industrial and commercial activity of Peru. It is considered the second industrial city in the country and has an important commercial center with close ties with other countries in the region.

Arequipa is known as the "White City", since it was built almost entirely in ashlar. It is located at 2,328 masl, in the desert mountains of the Andes, and is surrounded by 3 volcanoes: Misti, Chachani and Picchu Picchu. Without a doubt, it is the second most important city in the country, currently being an axis of economic development in southern Peru.

Arequipa's Gallery

Arequipa is a beautiful and vibrant city full of history, culture and unique gastronomy. On our site, you will find detailed information about the most popular tourist spots in the city, as well as recommendations for the best typical foods. From the famous Colca Canyon and the imposing Misti Volcano, to delicious local food such as stuffed rocoto and chactado guinea pig, you will discover everything you need to know to enjoy a unique and unforgettable experience in Arequipa.
