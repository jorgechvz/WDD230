Our History

Bountiful Foods was born from its founder's idea to create an organic and healthy food company that would provide nutritious and delicious options to the community. With a mission to offer fresh, locally grown products and a commitment to sustainability, Bountiful Foods began as a small store in Carlsbad, California. As the company grew, it expanded through food trucks that covered the entire California coast. Throughout the years, the company has remained committed to providing high-quality food and exceptional service to its community. From humble beginnings to its current position as a key player in the organic food industry, Bountiful Foods has maintained an unwavering dedication to health and wellness, and has achieved success that reflects its ethics and commitment to its customers.

We are in Santa Monica, California

At Bountiful Foods, we pride ourselves on offering fresh, healthy products that are grown locally in and around the Santa Monica area. Located in the heart of the city, our store offers a wide variety of fruits, vegetables and other seasonal products, as well as healthy and delicious food options that satisfy all tastes. We love being a part of this vibrant and dynamic community, and we strive to provide exceptional service to our customers.

Our Commitment

At Bountiful Foods, we are committed to healthy eating and organic produce. We believe in the value of providing fresh, nutritious food to our community and to tourists visiting the Santa Monica, California area.
