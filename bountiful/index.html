Bountiful Foods in Santa Monica, California

Santa Monica is a coastal city located in the western part of Los Angeles, California. It is situated on Santa Monica Bay and bordered by the Pacific Palisades to the north, Venice to the south, and Brentwood and Westwood to the east. The city has a population of approximately 92,000 people and is known for its beautiful beaches, iconic pier, and vibrant downtown area. But did you know that Santa Monica is also home to a thriving health food scene?

Bountiful Foods is proud to be a part of this community, offering fresh, organic produce and healthy meal options to the people of Santa Monica. Whether you're looking for a refreshing green juice to start your day or a satisfying smoothie to power you through your afternoon, we've got you covered.
So if you're in Santa Monica and looking for healthy, delicious food options, come visit us at Bountiful Foods. We're located in the heart of downtown and can't wait to serve you!
