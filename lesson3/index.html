Arequipa Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Purpose

The purpose of the Arequipa Chamber of Commerce is to be able to help people in the city to improve both their study and work skills as well as in their businesses in order to grow the professional network and help the community. Also help provide tools and resources to expand the reach of local businesses to increase economic growth in the city by boosting tourism, places to have fun, shops, events, etc.
